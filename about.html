A few weeks after I started learning python, a friend of mine introduced me to web development and I immediately became interested. I left python to learn javascript.

That was how my web development journey started, I attended a 3-months software engineering bootcamp at the university of lagos. Since the completion of the bootcamp, I have been learning non-stop .
